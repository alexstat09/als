How to export from Strong

In the Strong app open Settings → Export Data → Export, then share the CSV to yourself (AirDrop, Files, email). Open it here with Choose strong.csv, or paste its text above. Strong's column names line up with this app's exercises, so most lifts map automatically. Re-importing is always safe — sessions you've already imported are skipped.
