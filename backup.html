What's inside

The backup captures every synced data store: training (workouts, PRs, weights, templates), nutrition & meals, caffeine, supplements & water, sleep, identity (habits · journal · north star), measurements, bills, movies, improve, ideas, finance & net worth, goals, meal plans, and Nova's memory, plus your local preferences.

It's plain JSON: human-readable, no account, nothing leaves your device except the file you download. Restore is additive-safe: your existing cloud rows are only touched for the data the backup actually contains.
