What you're actually choosing

The eyes are the whole question. Nova with eyes is a companion — she's someone. Nova without is an instrument — she's something you read. Both are defensible; they're different products. Everything else you've chosen over the last year (Instrument Serif, line icons, no emoji, Pulse) points at the instrument. But your mum uses this app, and warmth is not a weakness.

If you want my pick: Pulse. It's the only one that answers "why does Nova look like that?" with something true — she's the app's heartbeat, the same waveform as your icon, drawn once and set in motion. The Aperture is the most premium and the most cold. The Gem is the safest upgrade and the least brave. The Companion is the best version of what you have now, if the face is worth keeping.
