How to copy from MyFitnessPal

On the MFP website open your diary, select a date range, and copy the page text (or use the diary's print/copy). Paste it above — the format with date headers, meal sections, and food rows is what this reads. You can paste many months at once; re-pasting is safe (duplicates are skipped automatically).
